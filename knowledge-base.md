# EpicChain Knowledge Base

## Introduction: What is EpicChain?
EpicChain (EPIC) is a pioneering Web3 Fintech platform building a Real-World Assets (RWA) layer. We bridge the gap between traditional finance and decentralized technology by offering tokenized Dubai real estate with 8%+ yield, and the Epic One card which allows users to spend crypto with up to 8% XRP cashback. Our infrastructure is powered by Ripple's XRP Ledger for maximum stability and efficiency.

## 1. Brand Essence

- **Type:** Debit and credit card.
- **Cashback:** Up to 8% XRP cashback.

### Multi-Currency Off-Ramp
- **Currencies:** Direct Rails to AED, USD, and INR.
- **Methods:** Cash pickup, bank deposit, or check.
- **Speed:** Same-day, 24/7.
